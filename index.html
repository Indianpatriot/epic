Our Ceremonies

Haldi Ceremony

A vibrant pre-wedding ritual where turmeric paste is applied to the bride and groom for blessing and beautification.

Sangeet Night

An evening of music, dance, and celebration where both families come together before the wedding day.

Mehndi Ceremony

Intricate henna designs are applied to the bride's hands and feet in this artistic pre-wedding celebration.

Ganesh Puja

Beginning any auspicious occasion with prayers to Lord Ganesha for blessing and removing obstacles.

About Us

Epic Events brings the soul of Indian celebrations to life — vibrant, heartfelt, and unforgettable. From dreamy weddings and traditional ceremonies to modern parties and corporate events, we blend culture with creativity in every detail. With a passion for planning and a deep respect for rituals, we craft experiences that reflect your story and style. At Epic Events, every moment is designed to feel truly special — just the way India celebrates.
